Expectations vs Reality in College





Expectations






  • The campus will be big and beautiful
  • There will be a good ratio of boys to girls
  • Mess food will be of excellent quality
  • Everyone will get high speed Wi-Fi connections
  • There will not be high pressure of studies
  • There will be all sports available with their respective grounds
  • There will be a canteen in the campus














Reality






  • The permanent campus is not complete and temporary campus is small in size
  • There is very bad ratio of boys to girls
  • Mess food is below average and always taste alike
  • There is one Jio Fibre connection with very poor connection speed
  • There are lot of assignment given and attendance is very strict
  • Not even proper sport equipment is being given to students
  • There is not even a single shop in the campus
